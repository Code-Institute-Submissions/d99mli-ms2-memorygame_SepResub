How to play

Start by clicking the start button.

A random colored button will be highlighted and your response is to click the same button. That finishes round 1..

Round 2 and all subsequent rounds begin with the previous button repeating and then adding a new random highlighted button. Your mission is to remember the ever growing sequence of highlighted buttons and replicate them.

When you press a wrong button, of sequence, the game is over and you will have to start all over again!

Challenge your family and friends to this addictive game to learn who has the best memory!
